Nadine Lustre is a famous Filipino actress, dancer, host, and singer. Nadine Lustre is known as the lead role of Diary ng Panget. She was also part of the girl band Pop Girls.

Early life

On October 31, 1993, Nadine Alexis Paguia Lustre was born in Quezon City, Phillippines. As of now, she is 30 years old and her birth sign is a Scorpio. She was born to her father Ulysses Lustre and her mother Myraquel Paguia-Lustre. And, she has siblings named Isaiah, Ezequiel, and Naomi.
