How Old Is Denji in Chainsaw Man?

Chainsaw Man is a popular manga and anime series that follows the adventures of Denji, a young man who merges with his pet devil Pochita and becomes a hybrid known as Chainsaw.

The series is set in a world where devils are born from human fears and can be hunted or contracted by devil hunters.

Denji joins the Public Safety Devil Hunters, a government agency that fights against devils, and encounters various enemies and allies along the way.

But how old is Denji in Chainsaw Man? In this article, we will answer this question and some related ones.

How Old Is Denji When He Met Makima?

Denji claims to be 16 years old when he first meets Makima, the leader of the Tokyo Special Division 4 of the Public Safety Devil Hunters.

Makima recruits Denji after he survives a betrayal by the Yakuza, who are using him as a devil hunter to pay off his father’s debt.

Makima offers Denji a better life in exchange for his loyalty and service as a devil hunter.

Denji agrees, hoping to impress Makima and fulfil his simple desires.

How Old Are Makima?

Makima’s age is never explicitly stated in the series, but she is implied to be much older than she looks.

She is revealed to be the Control Devil, one of the four Horsemen Devils that represent the primal fears of humanity.

As the Control Devil, Makima has the power to manipulate anyone who fears her, as well as resurrect herself after death.

She also has a long history of manipulating and killing Chainsaw Men throughout history, erasing their memories and existence from the world.

Therefore, Makima’s age could be centuries or even millennia.

How Old Is Denji in Ep 1?

In the first episode of the anime adaptation of Chainsaw Man,  Denji is still 16 years old.

The episode shows:

  • how Denji lives a miserable life as a devil hunter for the Yakuza
  • how he meets Pochita, a chainsaw-like devil dog who becomes his friend and partner
  • how he transforms into Chainsaw Man after being killed by the Zombie Devil

The episode ends with Denji meeting Makima for the first time and being recruited by her.

Is Chainsaw Man Really 16?

Denji’s age as Chainsaw Man is somewhat ambiguous, as he is not a normal human anymore.

He is a hybrid of a human and a devil, with Pochita acting as his heart.

As such, he has enhanced physical abilities, such as superhuman strength, speed, durability, and regeneration.

He can also transform parts of his body into chainsaws by pulling a cord on his chest.

However, he still retains his human personality, memories, and emotions.

He also ages normally, as shown by his turning 17 in Chapter 81 of the manga.

Therefore, Chainsaw Man’s age can be considered the same as Denji’s age.
