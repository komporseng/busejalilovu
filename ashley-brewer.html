Who is Ashley Brewer ?

Ashley Brewer is an American journalist. Since 2020, she has been an ESPN anchor and host/co-host. Brewer is the co-host of ESPN’s digital program Replay. Brewer has been hosting ESPN’s flagship shows, such as SportsCenter. Before joining ESPN, she worked for ABC7, KGUN-9, and Cox 7.

Brewer attended Chaparral high school (Arizona) in Phoenix, Arizona. She was a 12-time prep All-American swimmer as well as the Arizona 4A state champ in the 100m backstrokes in 2009. The University of Southern California was her next stop. She was part of the women’s varsity swimming team. She graduated in 2014.
