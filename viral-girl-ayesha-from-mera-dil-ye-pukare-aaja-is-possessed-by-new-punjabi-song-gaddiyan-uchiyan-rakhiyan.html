Viral girl Ayesha from Mera Dil Ye Pukare Aaja is possessed by new punjabi song Gaddiyan Uchiyan

 Posted on September 5, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  198 words  |  Larita Shotwell

A viral Pakistani girl named “Ayesha” recently posted a video on Instagram, where she expressed her current fascination with a new punjabi song ‘Gaddiyan Uchiyan Rakhiyan’.

Unless you’ve been living under a rock, you’ve probably seen the viral video of Ayesha dancing to the song “Mera Dil Ye Pukare Aaja” at a wedding function. This video took the internet by storm, with numerous edits circulating on social media.

The song and Ayesha’s captivating dance moves became the talk of the town online. Since then, there has been no turning back for this teenager, as her popularity soared after the video went viral.

Recently, Ayesha shared another video on Instagram. In the video, she can be seen wearing a white embroidered salwar suit while the song ‘Gaddiyan Uchiyan Rakhiyan’ by Riar Saab and Abhijay Sharma played in the background. In the caption, she mentioned that she is currently obsessed with this song.

“I am obsessed with this song,” the video caption reads.
