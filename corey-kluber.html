Career And Achievements

Corey Kluber has been in MLB since 2011 and has achieved a lot of success on the field. He has a good reputation for his injuries, which affects his performance. His achievements are still pretty impressive, considering that.

His current record is 113 wins and 71 losses, and his earned run average (ERA) is 3.31. As per stats published on MLB's official website, in 2022 alone, his win rate hasn't been the best as he is currently on ten wins and ten losses, but his ERA is 4.34, and his IP of 164.0. The pitcher has 1683 strikeouts in his career.

Kluber's time at Cleveland Indians was impressive, as he won two 'Cy Young Awards.' He won his first one in 2014 and the second one in 2017.
