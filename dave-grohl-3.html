Dave Grohl also known as David Eric Grohl is an American musician, singer, songwriter and record producer. As of 2023, Dave Grohl’s net worth is $330 million. He first gained prominence as the drummer for the grunge band Nirvana, and later as the band’s founder, primary songwriter, guitarist, and lead singer.

He is also the drummer and co-founder of the rock band Them Crooked Vultures, as well as a member of the short-lived Late! and Probot side projects. Grohl has also collaborated with Queens of the Stone Age on recordings and tours.
